# OPERA-DISP Data Licensing

## Access Requirements

- NASA Earthdata Login (free): https://urs.earthdata.nasa.gov
- Must authorize the ASF DAAC application in your Earthdata profile
- Must fill in "Study Area" profile field
- Authorize app at: https://urs.earthdata.nasa.gov/approve_app?client_id=BO_n7nTIlMljdvU6kRRB3g

## Sentinel-1 Data License (applies to OPERA-DISP products)

OPERA-DISP-S1 products are derived from Copernicus Sentinel-1 data. Usage is
governed by EU law (Regulation (EU) No 377/2014 and Commission Delegated
Regulation (EU) No 1159/2013).

**Key terms:**

- Free, full and open access — no restrictions on use including commercial
- No express or implied warranty including as regards quality and suitability
- User renounces any claims for damages against the EU and data providers

**Required attribution when distributing or communicating to the public:**

- For raw data: "Copernicus Sentinel data [Year]"
- For modified/derived products: "Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel data [Year]"

**Our attribution requirement:**

Since we derive velocity classifications from Sentinel-1 displacement data,
all outputs must include:

> Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel data (2016-2025), processed by
> NASA JPL OPERA project. InSAR displacement products accessed via
> ASF DAAC (doi:10.5067/SNWG/OPL3DISPS1-V1).
